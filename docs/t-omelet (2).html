Simple Omelet

Seven ingredients, loads of veggies, and simple methods. Hearty, savory, fluffy, and delicious.

Ingredients

Omelet

  • 5 ounces (~3/4 cup) sausage or bacon, diced
  • 2 Tbsp hummus
  • 2 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp paprika

Filling

  • 1 heaping cup veggies of choice (onion, tomato, mushroom, spinach are all excellent choices)

Toppings

  • Fresh herbs
  • Salsa
  • Parmesan cheese

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cook time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Prep veggies, cook sausage/bacon, and mince garlic. Set aside.
  3. Heat a small to medium, oven safe skillet over medium heat. Once hot, add olive oil and minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until just lightly golden brown.
  4. Transfer garlic to food processor, and beat eggs in a large bowl while adding the rest of the omelet ingredients. Set aside.
  5. To the still warm skillet over medium heat, add a bit more olive oil and the veggies. Season with salt and pepper and saute to desired doneness. It is easiest to start with onions and tomatoes, then add mushrooms, and end with spinach so each has proper time to cook. Set aside.
  6. Remove skillet from heat and make sure it's coated with enough oil so the omelet does not stick. Add back 1/4 of the veggies and spoon on the omelet batter, spreading it gently with a spoon or rubber spatula, being careful not to tear or cause gaps. The thinner and more evenly you can spread the better. You may not end up using it all.
  7. Cook over medium heat on the stove top for 5 minutes until the edges start to dry. Then place in 375 degree oven and bake until dry and deep golden brown (10-15 minutes). The longer it bakes the less soft/wet it will be.
  8. Using an oven mitt, in the last few minutes of cooking carefully add remaining veggies back on top of the omelet and cook another 1-2 minutes to warm through.
  9. Carefully remove from oven with oven mitt and fold over gently with a spatula. If it won't fold, serve as a frittata or scramble!
  10. Serve with desired toppings (salsa, parmesan cheese, cilantro, and toast recommended).
